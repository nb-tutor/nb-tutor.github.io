The greek alphabet app

I will be creating a premium app to help people master the Greek alphabet. The app will include:

  • Slides with all the letters and their pronounciation.
  • Examples with corresponding letters from the english language(when possible) to help you better grasp the pronounciation
  • Extra examples for each letter all pronounced by a native speaker.
  • Interactive games to help you master what you've learned in a fun way.
  • Answers to common questions such as
    • Why are some letters pronounced the same.
    • What is the correct pronounciation modern Greek or Erasmian.
    • How to know when to use which letter in a word.
  • Grammar section with detailed explanations
